About Me

Hi there, welcome to my portfolio! I am Raju, a game designer born and raised in Toronto, Ontario.
I am currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree at York University's Lassonde School of Engineering,
majoring in Digital Media
Game Arts.

Growing up, I had an immense passion for video games. It was one of the few ways I bonded
with my family or unwound after a stressful day. If I got stuck on Super Mario Bros.,
I’d hand the DS to my mom, and if I was getting crushed in Call of Duty with my siblings,
we’d still have a great time laughing about it. No matter what, games always brought a smile to our faces.
Now, having the opportunity to create the very thing I love most,
I’m excited to bring that same joy to others—just like games did for me!

If you have any further questions about me or my work, please feel free to contact me :)
