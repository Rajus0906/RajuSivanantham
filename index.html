About Me

Hi there! Welcome to my portfolio I am Raju, a game designer born and raised in Toronto, Canada.
I am currently studying at York University's Lassonde School of Engineering, majoring in Digital Media Game Arts for my bachelor's degree.

Growing up, I always had an immense passion for playing video games.
It was one of the few ways I was able to spend time bonding with my brothers and parents or if I just wanted to release stress,
If I got stuck on Mario I would hand the DS to my Mom, or if it was playing Call of Duty with my siblings and losing miserably,
it was always able to put a smile on our faces. Now being able to persure making the thing I love most, I wish
to be able to bring the same enjoyment to others the same way it did for me!

If you have any questions please feel free to contact me :)
